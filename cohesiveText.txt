Topic refers not to the grammatical subject of a sentence, but to its 'psychological' subject, and we expect to find the topic in the first few words of the sentence. Let's talk about robots. Also, did you hear about natural language processing? Software development is rad. Readers are more comfortable with these early topics because it helps them understand what the sentence is about. More important, readers depend on seeing in a sequence of topics (in a sequence of sentences) what the whole passage is about.
